Mindfulness for beginners

Mindfulness is a mental state achieved by focusing your awareness
on the present moment, while accepting one's feelings, thoughts,
and bodily sensations.

Common mindfulness techniques

  • Deep breathing: Inhale through your nose to a count of four, hold for one second, and exhale through your mouth to a count of five.
  • Body scan: Lie down and focus on each part of your body, noticing any sensations.
  • Walking meditation: Walk at a natural pace and pay attention to your breath, sights, and sounds.
  • Mindful eating: Be aware of the flavours, textures, and tastes in each bite.
  • Mindful listening: Practice attentive listening to concentrate on others without interruption.
  • Mindful stretching: Pay attention to the sensation of your muscles stretching and releasing.
