How to get started with Yoga

Yoga is a practice that combines physical postures, breathing
exercises, and meditation to improve health and well-being.

Common yoga techniques

  • Corpse pose: A resting pose that helps quiet the mind and cool the body after exercise. Props can make this pose more comfortable.
  • Tree pose: A pose that can help improve balance and alleviate anxiety and depression.
  • Child's pose: A resting pose that stretches the hips, thighs, and back, and calms the mind.
  • Downward-facing dog: A pose that creates length through the body and builds strength in the shoulders and arms. It can also help with headaches and calm the nervous system.
  • Standing forward bend: A forward fold that can help with flexible hamstrings and a healthy spine.
  • Mountain pose: A grounding pose that helps with good posture and strengthens back muscles.
  • Half forward bend: A flat-back forward bend that is often part of a sun salutation sequence.
